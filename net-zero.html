Flow Control for a Net Zero World

The UK has pledged to reach Net-Zero carbon emissions by 2050. Actuation Lab are supporting the energy transition journey through our novel techniques and processes. To this end, the company has been awarded a SBRI Sustainable Innovation Fund project, supported by Bristol Water and the OGTC. This project is focussing on developing a flow control technology to reduce greenhouse gas leaks on gas distribution networks, prepare for green-hydrogen transport and reduce energy use in the utilities sector.
