ACTUATION LAB APPOINTS DR STEVE KITSON AS CHAIR

  • 18/03/2021

Actuation Lab are pleased to announce another milestone in their continued growth with the appointment of Dr Steve Kitson as Chair.

Simon Bates, CEO, said "We are delighted to welcome Steve to the team at Actuation Lab. Steve has worked closely with us as a trusted advisor for the last 12 months, and this formal appointment allows us to mature our organisation and company direction. Steve has a wealth of experience in aiding technology companies achieve their potential and we are very much looking forward to working with him to help shape Actuation Lab's future."
