Harnessing the Potential of Complex Structures to Create Powerful Artificial Muscles

Actuation Lab is taking a revolutionary approach to the creation of fluid powered linear motion. By applying complex origami inspired designs we are able to create single component actuators from a diverse range of materials and processes. The excellent power-to-weight of these designs lends them to a wide range of applications, include in emerging robotics and aerospace systems. We are launching with two versions of our actuator, a composite version for use with high-performance hydraulics system, and an accessible, fully customisable 3D printed version for low pressure pneumatic systems.
