Careers

Actuation Lab are currently hiring! We are looking to fill the following positions:


Mechanical Design Engineer

Actuation Lab are looking for an engineer to join our team who is prepared to stretch themselves through this exciting stage of early company growth. We are looking to appoint a full-time mechanical design engineer, who will take a pivotal role in developing future flow-control products. Applicants must have a passion for building radically new technology from the ground up, be detail oriented and have a methodical approach to technical problem solving. The successful applicant will champion robust and consistent design practices within the company. Early focus for this role will be to work with the engineering team at their base in Bristol to design novel mechanical and electromechanical flow control products, such as valves and actuators.

The successful applicant will be provided with development opportunities to grow technically and professionally as the company scales. This will include training in composite material design and manufacture, and the opportunity to attend and present at international tradeshows and conferences.

Actuation Lab embraces flexible working practices, while also believing in fostering a strong company culture through team interaction and group events. A successful applicant will be expected to spend most of their time working onsite with the team in Bristol. Actuation Lab currently operates out of the National Composites Centre at the Bristol and Bath Science Park.

Tech Start-up Administrator

We are looking for an enthusiastic and adaptable individual to join our team as an Administrator on a part-time basis (up to 20 hours a week), with the option to grow into a full-time role as the company scales this year. The successful candidate for this role will have impeccable organisational skills, be confident, be creative, be willing to suggest better ways of working and be excited by new technology. Actuation Lab embraces flexible working practices, while also believing in fostering a strong company culture through regular in-person interaction and events. We can accommodate the successful applicant at our offices at the National Composites Centre in Bristol, but we will happily work with you to arrange the most mutually beneficial amount of remote working. We can as accommodate flexible working hours to suit your needs.
