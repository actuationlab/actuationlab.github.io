Actuation Lab are currently hiring! We are looking to fill the following position:


Associate Research & Development Engineer

Are you a current student or recent graduate from the University of Bristol from Engineering or Science looking for a Summer Internship? Actuation Lab are looking for an Associate R&D Engineer Intern who has a passion for building mechanical hardware and getting hands on. This 6-week paid position will begin in June 2021 and see you work alongside our team developing hardware for next-generation actuators.

As a valued member of our team, you will have the opportunity to:

  • Work with the very latest design software.
  • Get hands-on building components from a range of materials, including advanced composites.
  • Carry out rapid prototyping and 3D printing.
  • Independently carry out empirical testing, the results of which will have a real effect on our engineering decisions.
  • Educate yourself about the commercial aspects of an early stage start-up.
  • Learn about exciting opportunities available to those from a STEM background who are entrepreneurially minded.
