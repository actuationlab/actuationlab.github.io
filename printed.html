Beta product launching in the summer of 2019, our online tool allows you to design custom actuators before downloading print files for their creation. Compatible with mid-range standard FFF/FDM style printers (heated bed recommended), this tool provides an accessible route to prototyping with high-performance pneumatic actuators.

Here we show the normalised force and displacement performance of our Printed (P) and Hydraulic (H) actuators on a Ashby Chart. The further an actuator is towards the top right of the chart, the greater it work output potential for a given weight of device. Further information on interpreting this chart can be found from Cambridge University and the RSP.
