27/04/2018

Actuation Lab was successful in receiving funding in the second round of the University of Bristol’s annual New Enterprise Competition (http://www.bristol.ac.uk/red/nec/ sponsored by Santander). The funding will be used to continue prototype development and to continuing visiting potential clients around the UK to further validate the business.

21/06/2018

Actuation Lab visited the International Robotics Showcase in Liverpool, the pinnacle event of UK Robotics Week 2018, to meet exhibitors and see the launch of white papers in Urban Automation & Transport as well as robotics in AgriTech. A highlight for us was meeting the team from React Robotics https://reactrobotics.com/ and their 3D printed ‘dog-bot’

14/09/2018

The Actuation Lab 3D printed actuator design tool was presented to researchers from a variety of UK universities. Was great to see interest in out technology from the 3D printing community.

08/11/2018

Actuation Lab was delighted to receive the runner’s up prize in the Bristol New Enterprise Competition, which will give us 12-months of membership to the world’s number one University business incubator, SetSquared . We would like to thank all the sponsors of the event, and congratulate the winners and colleagues (pictured) of ours in composite research, iCOMAT you can read all about the event here.

30/11/2018

Actuation Lab attended the Materials Research Society Conference in Boston last week. This is giant annual conference, and a must attend for those that work with novel materials. This year a specific symposium was held on materials for next generation robotics including some great talks of relevance to us. A highlight was meeting the team behind the HASEL actuators a very different technology to our own. We also had an opportunity to present to the Human Computer Interaction group at MIT, a great opportunity to get more opinions on our technology.

14/12/2018

Actuation Lab has started at SetSquared Bristol, based in Brunel’s historic Engine Shed, it is regarded at the world’s number one university linked business incubator https://www.setsquared.co.uk/ . As a new member we had the opportunity to do an introductory pitch at the festive connect event sponsored by Hazlewoods Innovation.
